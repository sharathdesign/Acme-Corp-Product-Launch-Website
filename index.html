Frequently Asked Questions

How does Acme Corp verify my skills and experience?

We use a combination of document verification, skill assessments, and work history checks to build a profile recruiters can trust on sight.

Will recruiters be able to find me without me applying?

Yes. Once your profile is verified, recruiters actively searching for candidates with your skills can discover and reach out to you directly — no application required.

Is my profile visible to all companies or only ones I choose?

You're in full control. You can set your profile to public, restrict it to specific industries or company sizes, or keep it private until you're ready to be discovered.

How is this different from LinkedIn or a regular resume?

LinkedIn profiles and resumes are self-reported — anyone can write anything. Acme Corp profiles are independently verified, so recruiters know what they see is real before they even reach out.

How long does it take to set up my verified profile?

Most users complete their profile in under 20 minutes. Verification typically takes 24–48 hours depending on the documents submitted, after which your profile goes live.
